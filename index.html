BCH Mobile Billboard – Mozambique

A BCH-powered ride service + mobile billboard driving real adoption on the ground
What this is:

A real vehicle used for transport and meetups that also acts as a moving BCH billboard. It accepts Bitcoin Cash (BCH) as payment for rides.

Why it matters:

In Mozambique, most people don’t discover crypto online — they discover it in real life.

This vehicle creates a full adoption loop:

• People see BCH → get curious
• They take a ride → pay in BCH
• They learn → and start using it

Impact so far:

• 5,000+ daily local impressions

• Meetup + onboarding support

• Real BCH usage in Maputo & Matola
