BCH Mobile Billboard – Mozambique

A BCH-powered ride service + mobile billboard driving real adoption on the ground
What this is:

A real vehicle used for transport and meetups that also acts as a moving BCH billboard. It accepts Bitcoin Cash (BCH) as payment for rides.

Why it matters:

In Mozambique, most people don’t discover crypto online — they discover it in real life.

This vehicle creates a full adoption loop:

• People see BCH → get curious
• They take a ride → pay in BCH
• They learn → and start using it

Growing Impact:

This is not just visibility — it's the foundation of a local BCH circular economy.

• 5,000+ daily impressions across Matola & Maputo
• BCH accepted for real transport (rides paid in BCH)
• Direct onboarding through everyday interactions
• Meetups supported and expanded through mobility
• Ongoing outreach to onboard local businesses

The vehicle connects people, payments, and businesses — turning BCH from something people hear about into something they actually use.
